Social Trading

Connecting Users

2023 | Design | UX/UI | evaluate.xyz ↗

Introduction

We designed a robust social trading application for Evaluate, which is essential to its core purpose and functionality, providing users with an engaging and secure experience.


Design

I. Core Trading Interface

The primary trading view was designed to surface the most important information at a glance—active listings, user reputation, and real-time chat. Every element was considered for both clarity and trust.

II. Social Features

Live chat, user profiles, and reputation scores were woven into the trading flow—not bolted on as afterthoughts. The social layer makes trades feel less transactional and more collaborative.

Final Thoughts

Social trading requires a careful balance between information density and approachability. This project pushed me to think about trust signals and how design can make users feel safe when dealing with strangers online.
