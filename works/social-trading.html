Social Trading

Connecting Users

2023 | Design | UX/UI | evaluate.xyz ↗

Introduction

We designed a robust social trading application for Evaluate, which is essential to its core purpose and functionality, providing users with an engaging and secure experience.


UX & UI

I. Trade and Chat

The trading platform with integrated chatting allows users to trade NFTs live while chatting with their trading partner. It includes a user-friendly trade table system for selecting and swapping NFTs, with the option for asynchronous trading through offers with expiration dates for offline users.


II. Mobile

Converting the app to mobile meant optimizing the UX/UI design for smaller screens while retaining key features and design style. Resizing things to fit the mobile environment and still be legible was the focus.


III. Advanced Prototypes

We created an advanced prototype for the mobile design to help visualize the app's layout and functionality before development. It allows for user testing to gather feedback, ensuring the final product meets user needs and expectations.


Conclusion

In conclusion, I was thrilled to work with Evaluate.xyz and be a part of the team pioneering peer-to-peer NFT trading. Helping to solve these problems is what I most love to do and this project was as challenging as it was satisfying.
