Evaluate Website

Web Design and Motion

2023 | Design | UX/UI, Motion | evaluate.xyz ↗

Introduction

Evaluate.xyz is a p2p NFT trading application with a live chat feature. It operates on the Flow blockchain and can connect multiple different wallet types. I was on the team that designed the UX/UI for this app and website.


Landing Page & Website

I. The Landing Page

We designed the landing page to capture the attention of our audience and communicate our value proposition- that we are a social trading application built around the community of NFT collectors and the new web 3 technologies.


II. Emphasis Through Motion

We created a short looping video to help us to express a main feature of the app- The fact that you can trade across multiple collections on our platform. This is a novel ability we felt needed the proper attention to help attract users to our site.


III. 3d Elements

We used both textual and 3d elements to showcase the apps features. This helps people understand the usefulness of the app and its functions in a visually appealing way.


IV. Social Proof

We helped built trust and credibility with new users by adding social proof to the website. Showcasing users’ testimonials and positive reviews of the app helps new users feel confident in our product.


Conclusion

We aimed for simple, attractive design and clear messaging in creating this site. With careful improvements and attention to detail, we successfully launched a social trading app in this new Web 3 era.
