Shep

Bringing clarity and motion to a complex rebrand.

2025 | Illustration, Motion Design | Motion Design, Illustration, Animation | shep.io ↗

Opportunity

Shep (formerly Edisoft) helps businesses streamline their entire supply-chain process through a connected platform they call the Shep Fabric. The goal was to visually explain how their technology simplifies what's normally a messy, disjointed system—turning chaos into order through clear, approachable motion and design.


Contributions

I. Hero Animation

The hero piece centered on interaction with the Shep Fabric— a visual layer representing how Shep connects all moving parts of a business. The motion invites users to explore, showing how the fabric weaves data and processes together in real time.



II. Spaghetti to Blueprint

Transforms a tangled, chaotic system into a clean, structured diagram—Shep's core promise in one visual metaphor.



III. System Animations

A series of five short, connected animations built in Rive to explain how Shep's platform works.



  1. Automate – Demonstrates Shep's ability to remove manual effort by connecting systems and triggering actions automatically.

  1. Protect – Visualizes secure data handling and error prevention through stable, reinforcing motion cues.

  1. See Everything – Expands outward to show visibility across an entire network.

  1. Scale – Builds upward in motion and form, representing growth and adaptability.

  1. Launch Fast – Smooth, accelerating animation that captures how Shep speeds up deployment.

IV. Architecture of Modern Integration Illustrations

A three-part illustration series designed to visualize Shep's architecture principles.

  1. One Language – Represents Shep's ability to standardize communication across different tools and systems.

  1. Decoupled Systems – Illustrates how Shep separates dependencies between tools.

  1. Centralized Logic and Observability – Shows how Shep brings intelligence and oversight into a single view.

V. Technical Considerations

Final Thoughts

For Shep, I helped translate a complex platform and rebrand into simple, meaningful visuals that move. Built entirely in Rive, each animation balances clarity and personality—turning abstract ideas like automation, security, and scale into something users can instantly understand and connect with.

This project pushed me to grow across illustration, motion, and collaboration—refining how I approach iterative design, technical workflows, and client feedback. It's easily one of the most rewarding learning experiences I've had as a designer.
