Chat GPT Chrome Extension

UI Animation

2024 | Design, Motion | UX/UI, Motion

Opportunity

This idea came to me after drowning in tabs and screens doing research for another project—going back and forth from a source tab to ChatGPT and I thought, ‘Wouldn’t it be great if I could just stay focused in this window?’ And here we are!

Problem: Opening a separate window for ChatGPT disrupts workflow.

Solution: A Chrome extension that integrates ChatGPT into the browser.



Target Users and Use Case

I. Target Users

Busy Professionals: Get instant answers and writing assistance, improving efficiency without leaving your workflow.

Students: Get explanations and study help directly in your browser, streamlining research and assignments.

Researchers: Gather insights and generate ideas on the fly, speeding up the research process.


II. Use Case

Summarizing a long article or webpage.



Motion Design Rationale

III. Animation Goals

I wanted this animation to guide the user, highlight key moments, and create smooth transitions. I wanted it to feel like it was a part of the browser experience.



IV. User Flow

In this interaction, the user opens the extension, selects a prompt, then the extension interacts with the page.



V. Keyframes & Timing

My approach was to create custom easing curves for smooth, fluid timing that feels intuitive and natural for a better overall experience.



Design & Implementation

VI. UI & UX Design

I tried to compliment ChatGPT, and Chrome's UI by referencing them both in order to produce the final designs (shown below). I designed the extension to integrate seamlessly into the browser to create a harmonious feeling experience.




Spacial Design

VII. Exploring Spatial Relationships in UI Design and Motion

VIII. Technical Considerations



Final Thoughts

I created this design to seamlessly blend creativity with functionality, reflecting Microsoft’s user-centric and practical approach. Exploring innovative tools and techniques, I aimed to push boundaries and deliver a more impactful and meaningful user experience from Chat GPT.
