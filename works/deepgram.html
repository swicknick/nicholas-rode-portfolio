Deepgram

Interactive Motion Design for AI Voice Technology

2025 | Interactive Motion Design, Animation | Interactive Design and development, Motion Design | deepgram.com ↗

Opportunity

While exploring Deepgram’s capabilities, I wanted to highlight how its voice recognition and AI voice tools could feel in a real product environment. These motion pieces were designed to demonstrate how intuitive, interactive, and human-like the experience could be through thoughtful motion and interface design.



Key Contributions


  • Designed and implemented custom interactive Rive components used in key hero and product sections


  • Built scroll and hover-triggered animations optimized for web performance


  • Delivered production-ready assets


  • Ensured smooth playback across devices with reduced-motion and performance considerations


Case Studies

I. Voice-to_text AI Demo

Speech-to-text tools often feel static and disconnected from the user experience, making it difficult to visualize how they operate in real time. I created a dynamic UI animation that simulates a user speaking, triggering live transcription through an animated waveform and seamless text reveal. A subtle play button interaction introduces a natural flow between listening and viewing output.



II. "Voices" Interactive Experience

A playful, interactive animation highlighting Deepgram’s diverse AI voice library. Each avatar represents a unique voice style or accent—clicking an avatar plays a short demo, creating an engaging, character-driven preview of available voices.



III. AI Voice Assistant Ring Animation

A minimal, responsive animation of Deepgram’s voice assistant “ring.” The pulsing, reactive motion visually conveys listening and thinking states, giving the AI assistant a dynamic, intelligent personality.



IV. Saga Messages UI Walkthrough

A motion walkthrough illustrating Deepgram’s “Saga” platform integrated within Slack. The sequence (shown at the top of this page) demonstrates how users can send prompts, transcribe meetings, and summarize discussions directly in Slack—showcasing smooth in-app automation and collaborative workflow.

Final Thoughts

These animations were created to bring Deepgram’s technology to life through motion and interactivity—turning abstract AI capabilities into tangible, user-focused experiences. Each piece explores a different facet of their platform, from real-time transcription to dynamic voice interactions, emphasizing clarity, responsiveness, and human connection. My goal was to make the product feel intelligent yet approachable, bridging functionality and emotion through thoughtful design and motion.
