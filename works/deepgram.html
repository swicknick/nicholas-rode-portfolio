Deepgram

Interactive Motion Design for AI Voice Technology

2025 | Interactive Motion Design, Animation | Interactive Design and development, Motion Design | deepgram.com ↗

Opportunity

While exploring Deepgram's capabilities, I wanted to highlight how its voice recognition and AI voice tools could feel in a real product environment. These motion pieces were designed to demonstrate how intuitive, interactive, and human-like the experience could be through thoughtful animation and interaction design.


Contributions

I. Voice Waveform Interaction

An interactive waveform visualization that responds to simulated voice input in real time. Designed to feel responsive, alive, and precise—mirroring the speed and accuracy of Deepgram's API.

II. AI Voice Product UI

Motion design for a conceptual product UI built around Deepgram's voice tools. Transitions, state changes, and micro-interactions were choreographed to feel fluid and purposeful—showcasing how AI voice technology can enhance digital product experiences.

Final Thoughts

Deepgram's technology is genuinely impressive. This project was a chance to explore how motion design can make AI capabilities feel less abstract and more human. The goal was always to make the invisible visible—and to show how voice can be a natural, delightful input method.
